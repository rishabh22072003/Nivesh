📈 Market Directions
🟢 Bullish 📈 (तेजी)
🇮🇳 जब बाज़ार या स्टॉक ऊपर जा रहा हो — कीमतें बढ़ रही हों।
When the market or stock is going UP — prices rising and investors are optimistic.
📌 Example: ITC rose from ₹290 to ₹310 this week — clearly bullish.
💡 RSI > 50, price above SMA 50, MACD positive, high volume = bullish combo.
🔴 Bearish 📉 (मंदी)
🇮🇳 जब बाज़ार या स्टॉक नीचे जा रहा हो — कीमतें गिर रही हों।
When the market or stock is going DOWN — prices falling, investors pessimistic.
📌 Example: ITC fell from ₹330 to ₹300 after weak quarterly results.
💡 Signs: price below SMA 50, RSI below 40, MACD negative, high FII selling.
🟡 Neutral / Sideways (साइडवेज़)
🇮🇳 कीमत न ऊपर न नीचे — एक रेंज में चलती रहे।
Price moves in a flat range — market is undecided, neither up nor down.
📌 Example: ITC trading between ₹300–₹315 for 2 weeks = sideways.
💡 Wait for a breakout above resistance or below support before entering.
🚀 Rally (तेज़ उछाल)
🇮🇳 अचानक तेज़ी से कीमत ऊपर जाना।
A sharp, fast upward price move — often triggered by good news or strong results.
📌 Example: ITC rallied 5% after strong Q3 results.
💡 Rallies can be short-lived. Always check if fundamentals support the move.
📉 Correction (सुधार)
🇮🇳 ऊपर जाने के बाद थोड़ा नीचे आना — यह सामान्य है।
A temporary decline after a rise — healthy and normal. Usually 5–10%.
📌 Example: ITC rose to ₹380 then corrected to ₹340 before resuming uptrend.
💡 Corrections are buying opportunities in fundamentally strong stocks.
💥 Crash (क्रैश)
🇮🇳 अचानक बड़ी गिरावट — अक्सर पैनिक की वजह से।
A sudden 20%+ drop caused by panic, bad news, or global events.
📌 Example: Nifty crashed 30% in March 2020 due to COVID-19.
💡 Don't panic-sell in crashes. Strong stocks always recover over time.
📊 Technical Indicators
📏 RSI — Relative Strength Index
🇮🇳 RSI 0–100। 30 से नीचे = oversold (सस्ता)। 70 से ऊपर = overbought (महंगा)।
Momentum indicator on a 0–100 scale. Below 30 = oversold (buy zone). Above 70 = overbought (sell zone).
📌 Example: ITC RSI = 28 → oversold → potential bounce expected.
💡 Wait for RSI to rise back above 30 before buying — confirms the reversal.
📡 MACD
🇮🇳 MACD line signal line को ऊपर काटे = Buy। नीचे काटे = Sell।
12-day EMA minus 26-day EMA. Crosses above signal = bullish. Below = bearish.
📌 Example: MACD crossed above signal on ITC → Buy signal triggered.
💡 Works best in trending markets. Gives false signals in sideways markets.
📉 SMA — Simple Moving Average
🇮🇳 पिछले N दिनों की औसत कीमत। Price > SMA 50 = uptrend।
Average price over N days. Price above SMA = bullish. Classic: SMA 50 cross SMA 200 = Golden Cross.
📌 Example: ITC ₹304 > SMA 50 (₹295) = price above average = bullish.
💡 Golden Cross (SMA 50 above SMA 200) = strong long-term buy signal.
⚡ EMA — Exponential Moving Average
🇮🇳 हाल की कीमतों को ज़्यादा वज़न — SMA से तेज़ react करता है।
Like SMA but gives more weight to recent prices — reacts faster to changes.
📌 Example: EMA 20 reacted to ITC's drop faster than SMA 20.
💡 Use EMA for short-term signals, SMA for long-term trend identification.
🎯 Bollinger Bands
🇮🇳 तीन bands: मध्य SMA ± 2σ। ऊपरी = overbought। निचली = oversold।
Middle SMA ± 2 std deviations. Near upper band = overbought. Near lower band = oversold.
📌 Example: ITC touched lower Bollinger Band → historically bounces from here.
💡 Bands narrow before big moves (squeeze). Watch for breakout direction.
🧭 CCI — Commodity Channel Index
🇮🇳 +100 से ऊपर = strong uptrend। -100 से नीचे = strong downtrend।
Above +100 = strong uptrend. Below −100 = strong downtrend.
📌 Example: ITC CCI = −120 → strong downtrend signal.
💡 Nivesh AI uses CCI to confirm short-term buy/sell in the Technical tab.
💪 ADX — Average Directional Index
🇮🇳 ADX ट्रेंड की ताकत मापता है। 25+ = strong trend।
Measures trend strength (not direction). Above 25 = strong. Below 20 = weak/sideways.
📌 Example: ITC ADX = 32 → strong trend (could be up or down).
💡 ADX tells HOW STRONG. Use with RSI/MACD for direction.
📊 Volume (वॉल्यूम)
🇮🇳 कितने शेयर ट्रेड हुए। ज़्यादा वॉल्यूम = ज़्यादा विश्वास।
Number of shares traded. High volume + rise = strong bullish. High volume + fall = strong bearish.
📌 Example: ITC rose 3% on 2× average volume → strong conviction move.
💡 Price moves on low volume are unreliable. Always check volume to confirm.
🏗️ Support & Resistance
🇮🇳 Support = बार-बार उठता है। Resistance = बार-बार गिरता है।
Support = price where stock bounces up. Resistance = where it falls repeatedly.
📌 Example: ITC: strong support ₹295, resistance ₹325.
💡 Buy near support, sell near resistance. Broken resistance becomes new support.
📑 Fundamental Terms
💰 P/E Ratio — Price to Earnings
🇮🇳 P/E = कीमत ÷ कमाई। कम = सस्ता। ज़्यादा = महंगा।
Share Price ÷ EPS. Low P/E (<15) = undervalued. High P/E (>30) = expensive.
📌 Example: ITC P/E = 18.7 → fairly valued for FMCG.
💡 Never compare P/E across sectors. Compare only within the same industry.
💵 EPS — Earnings Per Share
🇮🇳 EPS = कुल कमाई ÷ कुल शेयर। जितना ज़्यादा, उतना अच्छा।
Net Profit ÷ Total Shares. Higher EPS = more profitable per share.
📌 Example: ITC EPS ₹16.25 growing 15% yearly → positive sign.
💡 Look for consistently growing EPS over 3–5 years, not just one quarter.
🏢 Market Cap
🇮🇳 Market Cap = कीमत × कुल शेयर = कंपनी की कुल कीमत।
Total value = Share Price × Total Shares. Large cap >₹20,000 Cr. Mid ₹5,000–20,000 Cr. Small <₹5,000 Cr.
📌 Example: ITC Market Cap ₹3.81T → Large cap, stable, lower risk.
💡 Large caps safer but slower. Small caps riskier but can give 10×.
📈 ROE — Return on Equity
🇮🇳 ROE = निवेशकों के पैसे पर return। 15%+ = अच्छा।
Net Profit ÷ Shareholders Equity. Above 15% = efficient use of investor money.
📌 Example: HDFC Bank ROE ~17% consistently → very efficient.
💡 Warren Buffett looks for ROE > 15% consistently over 10 years.
⚖️ Debt to Equity (D/E)
🇮🇳 D/E = कर्ज़ ÷ equity। 1 से कम = सुरक्षित।
Total Debt ÷ Equity. Below 1 = safe. Above 2 = high risk. Zero debt = safest.
📌 Example: ITC D/E = 0.51 → very manageable debt.
💡 For banking stocks D/E rules differ — they naturally carry high debt.
💸 Dividend Yield
🇮🇳 कंपनी मुनाफे का हिस्सा शेयरधारकों को देती है।
Annual Dividend ÷ Share Price × 100. Good passive income while holding the stock.
📌 Example: ITC dividend yield ~3.5% → good income while holding.
💡 High dividend + stable business = ideal for long-term income investors.
📊 Profit Margin
🇮🇳 हर ₹100 की बिक्री पर कितना मुनाफा। 15%+ = बढ़िया।
Net Profit ÷ Revenue. Above 15% = strong pricing power.
📌 Example: ITC profit margin 43.9% → exceptionally high.
💡 High margins mean pricing power. Benchmark: HDFC Bank, Asian Paints.
📐 Beta (बीटा)
🇮🇳 Beta = बाज़ार की तुलना में कितना ऊपर-नीचे।
Beta 1 = moves same as Nifty. >1 = more volatile. <1 = more stable.
📌 Example: ITC Beta ~0.5 → less volatile than Nifty — defensive stock.
💡 Low beta (FMCG, Pharma) safer in crashes. High beta (IT) better in bull runs.
🔮 Nivesh AI Signals
🎯 Consensus Signal — What Is It?
🇮🇳 Technical + Fundamental + Price Model — तीनों को मिलाकर एक फैसला।
Combines all three signals (technical, fundamental, model) into one honest recommendation.
📌 Example: Technical: Sell | Fundamental: Sell | Model: Buy → ⚠️ Mixed Signals.
💡 Never act on just one signal. Consensus is smarter — it considers all angles.
⚠️ Mixed Signals — What To Do?
🇮🇳 Technical Sell + Model Buy = conflict। इंतज़ार करो।
When Technical says Sell but model says Buy — do NOT buy. Wait for signals to align.
📌 Example: ITC: Technical Sell + Model +2% → Wait for RSI > 40 + MACD cross.
💡 In conflict: reduce position size, wait for 2 signals to agree before entering.
🟢 Buy Signal
🇮🇳 कम से कम 2 संकेत Buy = Consensus Buy। अच्छा मौका।
At least 2 of 3 signals agree stock will rise. Strongest when all 3 align + positive sentiment.
📌 Example: Technical Buy + Fundamental Buy + Model +3% = Strong Consensus Buy.
💡 Even on a Buy signal, use SIP — don't invest all at once.
🔴 Sell Signal
🇮🇳 2+ संकेत Sell = बेचना या नया निवेश न करना।
At least 2 of 3 signals say stock will fall. Don't buy new. Consider partial exit of existing.
📌 Example: Technical Sell + Fundamental Sell + bearish news = Strong Sell.
💡 Don't panic-sell everything. Review in 2–3 days — markets can reverse quickly.
🟡 Hold Signal
🇮🇳 Signals मिले-जुले = Hold। इंतज़ार करो।
No clear direction. Best action: don't buy more, don't sell existing. Wait for clarity.
📌 Example: Technical Hold + Fundamental Buy + Model +1% = Consensus Hold.
💡 Hold is the most common signal. Patience is your best strategy.
🔮 Price Prediction Models
🇮🇳 Nivesh AI: Linear Regression + ARIMA। Ensemble = सबसे भरोसेमंद।
Two models: Linear Regression (trend) + ARIMA (time-series). Weighted average = Ensemble — most reliable.
📌 Example: LR: ₹315 | ARIMA: ₹307 | Ensemble: ₹311 → use Ensemble.
💡 Models use past patterns — treat predictions as probability, not certainty.
📰 Sentiment Score
🇮🇳 Sentiment = खबरों का असर। +1 = बहुत अच्छा। -1 = बुरा।
Score −1 to +1 measuring news tone. Applied to adjust price prediction up or down.
📌 Example: Sentiment −0.40 → prediction reduced to reflect bad news.
💡 High negative sentiment + good technicals = caution. Wait for news to stabilize.
💰 Investment Concepts
🗓️ SIP — Systematic Investment Plan
🇮🇳 हर महीने थोड़ा-थोड़ा निवेश — ₹1000 हर महीने।
Invest a fixed amount regularly. Averages buying cost over time — removes timing pressure.
📌 Example: ₹5,000/month in ITC → more shares when price low, fewer when high.
💡 SIP is the safest strategy for volatile stocks.
🌍 FII / DII Flows
🇮🇳 FII खरीदे = बाज़ार चढ़े। FII बेचे = बाज़ार गिरे।
FII (Foreign) buying = bullish. FII selling = bearish. DII (Domestic) often cushions FII selling.
📌 Example: FII sold ₹5,000 Cr in March 2025 → Nifty fell 3%.
💡 Check FII/DII data daily on NSE website or Moneycontrol.
🛑 Stop Loss
🇮🇳 एक कीमत जिससे नीचे auto-sell — नुकसान सीमित करने को।
Pre-set price at which you auto-sell to limit loss. Essential risk management tool.
📌 Example: Buy ITC ₹310. Stop loss ₹295 (5%). Falls to ₹295 → auto-sell.
💡 Never invest without a stop loss. 5–8% is standard.
🌈 Diversification (विविधता)
🇮🇳 सारे पैसे एक स्टॉक में नहीं — अलग-अलग सेक्टर में बाँटो।
Spread across sectors (IT, FMCG, Banking, Pharma) to reduce risk.
📌 Example: 25% ITC + 25% TCS + 25% HDFC Bank + 25% Sun Pharma = well diversified.
💡 Ideal: 8–15 stocks across 4–5 different sectors.
🐂 Bull Run / Bear Market
🇮🇳 Bull Run = लंबे समय बाज़ार ऊपर। Bear = 20%+ गिरावट।
Bull Run = sustained rise over months/years. Bear Market = 20%+ fall from peak.
📌 Example: India bull run 2020–2024: Nifty 7,500 → 26,000.
💡 In bull run even bad stocks go up. In bear market good stocks fall — but recover faster.
📅 52-Week High / Low
🇮🇳 पिछले 52 हफ्तों में सबसे ऊंची और नीची कीमत।
Highest and lowest price in past year. Breaking 52W high = very bullish. Near 52W low = potential value.
📌 Example: ITC 52W High ₹528, Low ₹397. Currently ₹304 → below 52W low.
💡 Stocks near 52W lows can be value buys — but always check WHY they fell first.
🇮🇳 Indian Market Specifics
🏛️ NSE vs BSE
🇮🇳 NSE = Nifty 50। BSE = Sensex।
NSE has Nifty 50. BSE has Sensex. Most large stocks trade on both at nearly same prices.
📌 Example: ITC.NS = NSE. ITC.BO = BSE. Use .NS in Nivesh AI.
💡 NSE has higher liquidity and tighter spreads — prefer NSE for trading.
📊 Nifty 50 / Sensex
🇮🇳 Nifty = NSE के 50 सबसे बड़े। Sensex = BSE के 30 सबसे बड़े।
Nifty 50 = top 50 NSE companies. Sensex = top 30 BSE. They reflect overall market health.
📌 Example: Nifty above 22,000 = healthy. Below 19,000 = market stress.
💡 Check Nifty trend before making any individual stock decisions.
🏦 RBI & Repo Rate
🇮🇳 Repo Rate cut = सस्ते loan = बाज़ार चढ़े। Hike = महंगे loan = गिरे।
RBI controls monetary policy. Rate cut = cheaper loans = bullish. Rate hike = bearish.
📌 Example: RBI cuts 0.25% → home loan EMIs fall → banking stocks rise.
💡 RBI meets every 2 months — rate decisions move markets significantly.
💱 Rupee vs Dollar (USD/INR)
🇮🇳 रुपया कमज़ोर = IT को फायदा। Import वालों को नुकसान।
Weak rupee benefits IT exporters (TCS, Infosys) but hurts importers (oil, pharma).
📌 Example: USD/INR 83→87 → TCS gains, BPCL loses.
💡 Check Nivesh AI Global News for rupee movement and its impact on your stock.
🌍 Geopolitical Impact on India
🇮🇳 युद्ध, तेल की कीमत — ये सब भारतीय बाज़ार को प्रभावित करते हैं।
India imports 85% of oil. Middle East conflict → oil rises → inflation → RBI hikes → market falls.
📌 Example: Iran-Israel escalates → crude +10% → Nifty −3%.
💡 Always check Nivesh AI Global News before trading.
